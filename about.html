About Zealcorps.

With over 15 years of expertise in Building Automation in Electric and Industrial Automation, we have successfully completed a wide range of large-scale projects across commercial, residential, institutional, semi-conductor, and industrial sectors.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our industry certifications, including ISO 45001 and BizSafe Star. Backed by a dedicated team of over 40 professionals, we ensure high-quality solutions tailored to our clients' needs.

We are also recognized by the Building and Construction Authority (BCA) for overhead work, holding an ME02 Building Automation Level 2 certification.
